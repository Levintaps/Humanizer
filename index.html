Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Your Humanizer.

What is an AI text humanizer?
An AI text humanizer rewrites AI-generated content so it sounds like a real person wrote it. It removes robotic patterns, varies sentence structure, adds natural expressions, and eliminates tell-tale AI phrases like "It is important to note that…"
Is it completely free?
Yes! Your Humanizer is completely free. No account, no word limits per use, no paywalls. Just paste and humanize.
Can it bypass Turnitin, GPTZero, or Originality.ai?
Your Humanizer significantly reduces AI detection signals by transforming the underlying patterns detectors look for. The Heavy strength produces the most undetectable results. No tool can guarantee 100% — always review the output yourself.
Will it change the meaning of my text?
No. All factual content, statistics, numbers, citations, and core meaning are preserved exactly. Only the style and phrasing change. Use the Diff view to verify after each run.
What makes Thesis mode different from Academic?
Thesis mode strictly enforces third-person only (never "I" or "we"), no contractions, formal passive voice where appropriate, and hedging language ("suggests that", "appears to"). Academic Essay is more flexible and allows first person if the original uses it.
Is my text stored or shared?
No. Your text is sent to our AI service for processing and immediately discarded. We do not store, log, or retain any of your text. The API key is held server-side only — never exposed in your browser.
