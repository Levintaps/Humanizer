Questions answered

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Your Humanizer.

What is an AI text humanizer?
An AI text humanizer is a tool that rewrites AI-generated content to sound like it was written by a real person. It removes robotic patterns, varies sentence structure, adds natural expressions, and eliminates tell-tale AI phrases like "It is important to note that…" or overused connectors like "Furthermore" and "Moreover".
Is Your Humanizer completely free?
Yes! Your Humanizer is completely free to use. There's no account required, no word limits for individual uses, and no hidden paywalls. Just paste your text and humanize.
Can it bypass Turnitin, GPTZero, or Originality.ai?
Your Humanizer significantly reduces AI detection signals by transforming the underlying patterns that detectors look for. The Heavy strength mode produces the most undetectable results. However, no tool can guarantee 100% bypass of all detection systems — always review the output yourself before submitting.
Will it change the meaning of my text?
No. The AI is explicitly instructed to preserve all factual content, data, numbers, proper nouns, and the core meaning of your text. It only changes how the ideas are expressed, not what they say. Use the Diff view to verify changes after each run.
What AI model does it use?
Your Humanizer uses Meta's Llama 3.3 70B model running on Groq's infrastructure. This is one of the most capable open-source language models available, providing significantly better writing quality than smaller models. Groq is also free — meaning you get top-tier AI humanization at no cost.
Is my text stored or used for training?
No. Your text is sent to the AI API for processing and immediately discarded. We do not store, log, or retain any of your text content. The API key is held server-side so it's never exposed in your browser. Your writing is completely private.
