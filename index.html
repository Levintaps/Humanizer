What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies (Always Active)

  • Session Management: To maintain your session while using the tool
  • Security: To prevent cross-site scripting and ensure secure connections
  • Functionality: To remember your humanization settings within a session

2. Preference Cookies (Optional)

  • User Settings: To remember your preferred humanization options
  • Theme Preferences: To maintain your chosen display preferences
  • Language Settings: To remember your language preference

3. Analytics Cookies (Optional)

  • Usage Statistics: To understand how users interact with our service
  • Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix technical issues

You have several options for managing cookies:
